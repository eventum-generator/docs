The Overview page is the Studio home screen. It shows your Eventum version and platform info, provides quick actions for creating projects or browsing the [Eventum Hub](/hub), and links to the main Studio areas.

  lightSrc="/images/studio_ui/overview_page_light.png"
  darkSrc="/images/studio_ui/overview_page_dark.png"
  alt="Overview page in Eventum Studio"
  className="rounded-lg border mt-2"
/>

The **Recent projects** section at the bottom shows your most recently accessed generator projects for quick navigation to the [project editor](/docs/studio/projects).
